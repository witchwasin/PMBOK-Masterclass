## Teaching Notes

Do not accept a polished executive deck while canonical artifacts, owners or approval evidence are missing. Test facts against scenario master, especially ERP Working Budget 45 ล้านบาท versus Funding Envelope 60 ล้านบาท

## Model Standards

ERP model: canonical artifacts link charter/benefits, WBS-schedule-cost, acceptance, resource/RACI, status, risk/change, vendor acceptance and hybrid plan. Status may be Conditional Go pending reconciliation threshold

Hotel model: canonical artifacts link MVP outcome, backlog priority, flow evidence, payment/security acceptance, partner feedback, launch decision and operations handover. Status may be Conditional Go until payment resilience and operational incident readiness satisfy evidence

## Review Checklist

- [ ] Manifest links canonical artifacts rather than copies
- [ ] Every required artifact is at least `Usable`
- [ ] Scenario facts and assumptions are consistent
- [ ] ERP 45M/60M terms are not mixed
- [ ] Three decisions/trade-offs are defended
- [ ] Critical risks and readiness recommendation are explicit
- [ ] Comparative tailoring memo has rationale, triggers and cadence

## Scoring Rubric

| Dimension | Share | Usable Standard |
|---|---:|---|
| Completeness/traceability | 40% | Canonical artifacts trace to handover |
| Executive defense | 25% | Decision, trade-off, risk and readiness are defensible |
| Integration | 20% | Core controls are consistent |
| Comparative tailoring | 15% | Context, rationale and review are stated |

## Minimum Pass

Minimum score 70/100 and no critical contradiction in scenario facts, approval authority or artifact handoff.
