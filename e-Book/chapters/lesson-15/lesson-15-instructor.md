# Instructor Guide - Chapter 15

ลิงก์ไป Learner Chapter: [lesson-15-learner.md](./lesson-15-learner.md)

## Teaching Notes

สอน Agile เป็น disciplined feedback loop ไม่ใช่ permission to skip planning. แยก Scrum สำหรับ sprint increment กับ Kanban สำหรับ flow/support

## Model Answer

Preserve the sprint goal by moving promotion below payment resilience unless outcome evidence changes PO priority. Record board policy separately. Do not give PM, PO and team undifferentiated co-ownership of all Agile artifacts

## Review Checklist

- [ ] PO owns priority
- [ ] Delivery Team owns flow operation
- [ ] Sprint goal or WIP policy is explicit
- [ ] Definition of Done includes quality/acceptance
- [ ] Feedback evidence informs backlog
- [ ] Governance/risk controls remain visible

## Scoring Rubric

| Level | Description |
|---|---|
| Incomplete | Ceremony labels without ownership or feedback |
| Usable | Backlog, board and feedback evidence support delivery decisions |
| Professional | Ownership, feedback use and delivery reasoning are traceable |
