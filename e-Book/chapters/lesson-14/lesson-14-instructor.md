# Instructor Guide - Chapter 14

ลิงก์ไป Learner Chapter: [lesson-14-learner.md](./lesson-14-learner.md)

## Teaching Notes

Start with business need and risk allocation, not contract type. A vendor's completed task is not automatically business acceptance or authorized payment

## Model Answer

Return unclear SI customization request for scope clarification. Procurement Manager owns commercial evaluation; PM confirms delivery/acceptance impact; Legal and Finance review; Sponsor approves when threshold requires. Payment releases only after accepted deliverable and authorization

## Review Checklist

- [ ] Make-or-buy rationale links to need and risk
- [ ] Scope maturity informs contract type
- [ ] Acceptance criteria and payment evidence are connected
- [ ] Commercial owner and delivery owner are separate
- [ ] Change control and authority are clear
- [ ] Vendor risk feeds Agile/hybrid constraints

## Scoring Rubric

| Level | Description |
|---|---|
| Incomplete | Procurement decision lacks scope, acceptance or authority |
| Usable | Commercial, delivery and payment controls are connected |
| Professional | Risk allocation and change control enable defensible commercial decision |
