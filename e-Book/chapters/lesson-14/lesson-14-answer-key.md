## Expected Standards

Procurement decision must connect need, scope, risk allocation, contract control, acceptance evidence and payment authorization

ERP SI change request should be clarified and re-priced or approved as controlled change. Hotel vendor cases should consider SLA, security, payment reliability, integration and launch readiness

Minimum score 70/100 and all artifact dimensions at least `Usable`.
