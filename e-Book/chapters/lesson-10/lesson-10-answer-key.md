| Section | Share | Type |

## Expected Answers

Quality means conformance to requirements and fitness for use. Grade is level of feature or sophistication

QA checks whether the process is designed and followed. QC checks deliverable results and defects. Business acceptance confirms whether criteria are met for use

For green ERP UAT dashboard, required evidence includes coverage, severity/age, retest/regression, data reconciliation, environment readiness, key-user participation and signed acceptance

## Scenario Standards

ERP response should flag master data finance close defects as critical when they affect accounting/audit. Hotel response should treat payment failure 8% as release-readiness risk requiring mitigation, rollback/support plan and authority

## Common Wrong Answers

| Weak Answer | Why It Fails |
|---|---|
| Dashboard is green so go-live | No traceable evidence |
| Count defects only | Ignores severity and user impact |
| QA means testing | Confuses process assurance with result checking |
| Accept risk without owner | Residual risk has no accountable authority |

## Minimum Pass Standard

Minimum score 70/100 and all artifact dimensions at least `Usable`.
