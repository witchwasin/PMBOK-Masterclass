# Instructor Guide - Chapter 10

ลิงก์ไป Learner Chapter: [lesson-10-learner.md](./lesson-10-learner.md)

## Teaching Notes

สอน quality เป็น business readiness decision. Dashboard colour and defect count are signals, not decision evidence by themselves

## Learning Intent

ผู้เรียนต้อง trace requirement to test to result to acceptance และเสนอ go/no-go option พร้อม residual risk และ authority

## Suggested Timing

| Segment | Time |
|---|---:|
| Lesson 09 handoff | 10 |
| Quality/grade and QA/QC | 25 |
| Acceptance and test strategy | 30 |
| ERP/Hotel cases | 25 |
| Artifact workshop | 30 |

## Thinking Walkthrough

1. Start from scope and acceptance inputs
2. Define quality objectives and thresholds
3. Separate prevention, QA and QC
4. Trace requirement to test to result to acceptance
5. Assess defect severity and residual risk
6. Prepare go/no-go options and authority
7. Prepare operating handoff

## Completed Example

ERP finance acceptance needs coverage, critical close scenarios, reconciliation, retest, business owner sign-off and residual risk record. A green dashboard without these is insufficient

## Model Answer

Professional answer requests requirement/test coverage, severity and ageing, retest/regression, environment/data readiness, reconciliation, key-user participation and signed business acceptance

QA checks process, QC checks results, and Business Owner decides acceptance. These roles do not replace each other

## Review Checklist

- [ ] Quality objectives trace requirements/WBS
- [ ] Quality/Grade and QA/QC are separated
- [ ] Acceptance criteria have owner and evidence
- [ ] Test levels, entry/exit and severity are defined
- [ ] Residual risk and go/no-go authority are clear
- [ ] Schedule/cost support quality work

## Scoring Rubric

| Dimension | Incomplete | Usable | Professional |
|---|---|---|---|
| Traceability | test list ลอย | requirement to test | result to acceptance to handover ครบ |
| QA/QC | ปนกัน | แยก process/result | prevention, appraisal and failure cost clear |
| Test strategy | defect count | levels/entry/exit/severity | data/environment/regression/readiness ครบ |
| Decision | dashboard colour | go/no-go option | residual risk and authority clear |

## Artifact Approval Guidance

ผ่าน `Usable` เมื่อ quality pack ใช้ตัดสิน acceptance ได้ ผ่าน `Professional` เมื่อ traceability, residual risk, go/no-go threshold และ operation handoff ครบ
