## Expected Answers

Data is raw signal. Information is organized for meaning. Communication creates shared understanding and action. Status report reports. Decision brief requests a bounded decision with evidence and deadline

Push fits low-complexity distribution. Pull fits reference or dashboard information. Interactive fits complex, urgent, sensitive or ambiguous topics

Sponsor approves communication plan/cadence; PM approves recurring status reports

## Scenario Standards

ERP answer should turn data migration red into executive brief with cutover impact, options, recommendation, deadline and decision record

Hotel answer should tailor payment incident communication to PO, product team, marketing, hotel partners, customers and operations/support

## Common Wrong Answers

| Weak Answer | Why It Fails |
|---|---|
| Send same dashboard to all | Not tailored to decision need |
| Email sent means done | No acknowledgement/action evidence |
| Escalate without ask | Authority cannot decide |
| No decision log | Communication cannot feed Lesson 13 risk work |

Minimum score 70/100 and all artifact dimensions at least `Usable`.
