# Instructor Guide - Chapter 12

ลิงก์ไป Learner Chapter: [lesson-12-learner.md](./lesson-12-learner.md)

## Teaching Notes

สอน communication เป็น decision/action system. A sent email is not proof of understanding

## Learning Intent

ผู้เรียนต้องแปลง technical red status เป็น audience-specific communication and executive decision brief ที่บันทึก acknowledgement และ decision ได้

## Thinking Walkthrough

1. Start from approved RACI and audience
2. Identify decision or action needed
3. Convert data to information and recommendation
4. Choose channel by urgency, complexity and sensitivity
5. Set cadence and owner
6. Record acknowledgement, decision and escalation

## Completed Example

ERP data migration risk: Steering Committee gets one-page decision brief by Friday with cutover impact, three options and recommendation. Workstream gets daily defect detail. Sponsor approves executive cadence; PM approves this week's status report

## Model Answer

Use a targeted executive brief rather than a broader dashboard. State missing confidence information, owner and decision date. A colour-coded report is not an escalation unless it contains an explicit decision request and records response

## Review Checklist

- [ ] Audience and decision need are explicit
- [ ] Data, information, communication, status and decision brief are distinguished
- [ ] Channel matches urgency and complexity
- [ ] Cadence, owner and feedback loop are present
- [ ] Escalation has decision ask and evidence
- [ ] Handoff to Lesson 13 risk signals is clear

## Scoring Rubric

| Level | Description |
|---|---|
| Incomplete | Generic distribution list with no decision need or escalation path |
| Usable | Audience, message, channel, cadence and owner support decisions |
| Professional | Messages, decisions and escalations are traceable and adapted to stakeholder needs |

## Artifact Approval Guidance

ผ่าน `Usable` เมื่อ plan ใช้ส่งข้อมูลให้ถูกคนถูกเวลาและมี feedback loop ผ่าน `Professional` เมื่อ decision/escalation traceable และ feed risk management ได้
