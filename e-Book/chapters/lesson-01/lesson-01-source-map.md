## Claims Explicitly Not Made

- PMBOK guarantees project success.
- PMBOK is a fixed methodology.
- PMBOK equals Waterfall.
- Agile, Predictive or Hybrid is universally best.
- Go-live alone proves project value.
- ERP working budget equals total funding envelope.
- Hotel Booking launch alone proves the 18-month direct booking target.
