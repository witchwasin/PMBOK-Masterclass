| Level | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Incomplete | Separates project/operation by deadline only or stops at output |
| Usable | Separates boundary and connects Business Need to Outcome with owner |
| Professional | Adds evidence gap, transition ownership, benefit measure and governance recommendation |
