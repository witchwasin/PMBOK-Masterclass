# Answer Key — Chapter 04

Retrieval 10%; application/judgement/artifact 90%.

## Core Answers

Process Groups describe management timing/function. Knowledge Areas describe the subject being managed. Integration connects impacts into a decision.

For ERP approval workflow, strong answers include Scope, Schedule, Cost, Quality, Risk, Resource, Stakeholder, Communications, Procurement and Integration impacts.

For Hotel Corporate Booking, acceptable options include include now with adjusted baseline, defer, or replace lower-value scope. Recommendation must connect high season, outcome, missing evidence and approval authority.

Artifact review should reject generic statements such as “minor impact everywhere” when no owner, evidence, threshold or decision route is shown.

## Pass Criteria

Minimum score 70/100. Artifact must reach at least `Usable` in coverage, evidence, options, governance and handoff.
