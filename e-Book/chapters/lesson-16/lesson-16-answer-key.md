## Expected Standards

Predictive fits clear scope, high compliance and baseline control. Agile fits high uncertainty and fast feedback. Hybrid must be designed by workstream with rationale, trigger, cadence and authority

ERP answer should use predictive governance for migration, contract, budget and cutover while using iterative feedback for configuration/prototypes. Hotel answer should use Agile product cycles with predictive controls for payment, security and launch readiness

Minimum score 70/100 and all artifact dimensions at least `Usable`.
