# Instructor Guide - Chapter 16

ลิงก์ไป Learner Chapter: [lesson-16-learner.md](./lesson-16-learner.md)

## Teaching Notes

สอน tailoring เป็น context-driven control design. อย่าให้ผู้เรียนตอบด้วย label เช่น "Agile ทั้งหมด" หรือ "Waterfall ทั้งหมด" โดยไม่มี workstream rationale

## Model Answer

Recommend hybrid delivery by workstream, not Agile for the whole project. Document assumptions, architecture/security checks, launch gates, trigger thresholds and cadence. Send material governance changes to Sponsor/Steering Committee for approval

## Review Checklist

- [ ] Approach chosen by workstream
- [ ] Rationale uses uncertainty, risk, compliance, contract and feedback needs
- [ ] Trigger and cadence are explicit
- [ ] Governance owner and approval authority are named
- [ ] Evidence shows adaptive control
- [ ] Capstone readiness handoff is clear

## Scoring Rubric

| Level | Description |
|---|---|
| Incomplete | Method label only; no rationale or control |
| Usable | Workstreams have approach, trigger, cadence and authority |
| Professional | Workstream tailoring and evidence sustain executable hybrid plan |
