# Traceability — Chapter 05

## Artifact Contract

| Artifact | Owner | Approval |
|---|---|---|
| Project Charter | Sponsor | Sponsor |
| Governance and Decision Rights Map | Sponsor | Steering Committee Chair |
| Change Decision Record | Project Manager | Change Authority by threshold |

## Inputs and Outputs

| Direction | Artifact |
|---|---|
| Inputs | Value Chain, Lifecycle Map, PM Coverage and Cross-impact Map |
| Outputs | Project Charter, Governance and Decision Rights Map, Change Decision Record |
| Feeds | Lesson 06 Stakeholder Register and Engagement Strategy |

## Acceptance

`Usable` means Charter and governance can authorize work and route change decisions. `Professional` means objectives, authority, impacts, approval evidence and baseline updates are traceable.

## Handoff to Lesson 06

Lesson 06 uses Charter and Governance Map to identify stakeholders, influence, engagement needs and approval expectations.
