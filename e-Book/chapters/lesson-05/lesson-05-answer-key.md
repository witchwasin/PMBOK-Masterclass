## Core Answers

Integration Management connects charter, plan, execution, knowledge, monitoring, change control and closing into one decision system.

ERP approval workflow change must identify baselines, missing evidence, options, authority and post-decision updates. PM prepares analysis; CCB/Sponsor decides according to threshold.

Hotel promotion engine decision must weigh conversion value, high-season schedule, payment stability, resource capacity and support readiness.

Artifact review should flag missing business need, PM authority, impact analysis, options, approval evidence and baseline updates.
