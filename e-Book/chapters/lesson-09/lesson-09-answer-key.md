## Expected Answers

Cost estimate is a cost forecast for work. Cost baseline is the approved time-phased budget used for control. Total funding envelope is not the same thing as working delivery budget

For PV 20, EV 15, AC 18: CV = -3, SV = -5, CPI = 0.83, SPI = 0.75, EAC = BAC/CPI = 45/0.83 ≈ 54.2, VAC = BAC-EAC ≈ -9.2. Interpretation: over cost and behind schedule based on earned work, forecasting about 9.2M over the 45M working budget if the trend holds
