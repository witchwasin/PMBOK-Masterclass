## Teaching Notes

สอน cost เป็น performance conversation ไม่ใช่ accounting snapshot. ให้ผู้เรียนแยก funding, baseline, reserve, actual และ forecast ก่อนแนะนำ decision

## Thinking Walkthrough

1. Start from WBS and time-phased schedule
2. Separate 45M Working Budget from 60M Total Funding Envelope
3. Calculate PV, EV, AC, CPI and SPI
4. Ask cause and remaining-work evidence
5. Prepare options: approve, defer, de-scope, recover or reforecast
6. State authority and updates

## Completed Example

ERP PV 20, EV 15, AC 18:

- CV -3, SV -5
- CPI 0.83, SPI 0.75
- EAC = BAC/CPI = 45/0.83 ≈ 54.2, VAC = BAC-EAC ≈ -9.2
- Interpretation: over cost and behind schedule relative to earned work; at current trend the project forecasts about 9.2M over the 45M working budget
- Decision still needs cause, quality/data readiness, reserve status and benefit of change

## Model Answer

Professional answer must not use Total Funding Envelope 60 ล้านบาท to hide pressure on Working Budget 45 ล้านบาท. Change 2 ล้านบาท can be approve/defer/de-scope only with benefit, EAC/VAC, schedule/quality effect, authority and updated baseline/forecast evidence

## Review Checklist

- [ ] Funding, baseline, reserve, actual and forecast are separated
- [ ] EVM math and interpretation are correct
- [ ] 45M and 60M terms preserved
- [ ] Change recommendation includes benefit and authority
- [ ] Forecast update is stated
- [ ] Handoff to quality constraints is clear

## Scoring Rubric

| Dimension | Incomplete | Usable | Professional |
|---|---|---|---|
| Cost structure | ปน funding/baseline/reserve | แยกได้ชัด | governance threshold and reserve authority clear |
| EVM | คำนวณผิดหรืออ่านผิด | PV/EV/AC/CPI/SPI ถูก | explains cause and uncertainty |
| Decision | ดูตัวเลขเดียว | options and recommendation | benefit, EAC, quality/schedule impacts |
| Handoff | ไม่เชื่อม quality | มี cost constraints | prevention/testing budget implications clear |

## Artifact Approval Guidance
