## Expected Standards

Risk is uncertain; issue exists now. Every actionable risk needs trigger, owner, response, threshold and review evidence

ERP data quality should remain a monitored risk until trigger is reached; after trigger it becomes issue/escalation and may need Change Request. Hotel payment/security risks need response owners and launch triggers

Minimum score 70/100 and all artifact dimensions at least `Usable`.
