# Instructor Guide - Chapter 13

ลิงก์ไป Learner Chapter: [lesson-13-learner.md](./lesson-13-learner.md)

## Teaching Notes

สอน risk เป็น living decision process. ให้ผู้เรียนแยก uncertain event, trigger, issue, escalation และ change request เป็นคนละ field/event

## Model Answer

Keep an item as a monitored risk until the trigger is observed. When trigger occurs, create an issue, record escalation decision and submit Change Request if scope, schedule, budget or baseline must change. Routine Steering Committee approval is not required for every risk response

## Review Checklist

- [ ] Risk/issue/trigger/change are separated
- [ ] Each risk has owner, probability, impact and response
- [ ] Trigger is observable
- [ ] Reserve and risk acceptance evidence are named
- [ ] Escalation threshold is clear
- [ ] Handoff to procurement risk allocation is visible

## Scoring Rubric

| Level | Description |
|---|---|
| Incomplete | Vague risks have no trigger, owner or response |
| Usable | Risk-to-issue escalation and delegated approvals are traceable |
| Professional | Response, reserve, residual risk and change control are decision-ready |
