## Expected Answers

Strong answers identify dependency logic before recommending recovery. For ERP SIT delay, ask whether retest, migration rehearsal and UAT sit on the critical path and whether float exists

Acceptable recovery options include using float, resequencing, crashing, fast tracking, splitting release or rebaseline. Each option must state cost, quality, resource and risk effects

## Scenario Standards

ERP answer should preserve go-live before 1 ตุลาคม and working budget 45 ล้านบาท. Hotel answer should include timeline 8 เดือน, payment readiness, pilot, launch criteria and support readiness

## Common Wrong Answers

| Weak Answer | Why It Fails |
|---|---|
| เลื่อนทุกอย่าง 2 สัปดาห์ทันที | Does not test critical path or float |
| เพิ่มคนโดยไม่ดู skill | Ignores ramp-up and cost |
| Fast-track without quality gates | Raises rework and defect risk |
| No baseline authority | Cannot approve schedule change |
