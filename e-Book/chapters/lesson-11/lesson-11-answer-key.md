## Expected Answers

Resource Management makes people, skills, capacity, authority and physical resources ready when needed. A named person is not proof of capacity

RACI should have one Accountable role per decision. Functional Managers approve departmental capacity. PM approves the consolidated resource plan. Steering Committee handles exceptions and priority conflicts

## Scenario Standards

ERP answer must preserve 80 key users and identify BAU/month-end capacity conflict. Strong options include phased UAT, backfill, part-time allocation, overtime, vendor support, resequence or scope trade-off

Hotel answer should mention cross-functional team capacity, QA/backend bottleneck, regression testing, release cadence and support readiness

## Common Wrong Answers

| Weak Answer | Why It Fails |
|---|---|
| PM assigns finance users directly | Ignores functional manager authority |
| Multiple Accountable roles | Decision ownership is unclear |
| Add people only | Ignores skill, ramp-up and coordination |
| No capacity evidence | Plan cannot be executed |

## Minimum Pass Standard

Minimum score 70/100 and all artifact dimensions at least `Usable`.
