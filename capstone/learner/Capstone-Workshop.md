# Capstone Workshop

**Role:** Delivery lead preparing executive readiness recommendation.

- Select primary scenario and assemble the manifest links.
- Run Gate 1 before Gate 2; correct missing governance evidence first.
- Compare every fact against its Scenario Master and label assumptions.
- Trace a critical risk to response, issue, decision/change, acceptance and handover.
- Prepare a Go / Conditional Go / No-go recommendation and its decision owner.

**Output:** completed manifest, comparative memo and executive defense brief.
