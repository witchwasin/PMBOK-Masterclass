# Comparative Tailoring Memo Template

Write 3–5 pages about the non-primary scenario. Do not call a method “better” without context.

1. Context, uncertainty, constraints and value feedback.
2. Workstream-level predictive/agile/hybrid choices.
3. Rationale, assumptions, trigger and review cadence.
4. Governance: PM integration, PO product policy, reviews and approval authority.
5. Effects on risk, acceptance, procurement, launch and handover.
6. Recommendation and evidence required before release.
