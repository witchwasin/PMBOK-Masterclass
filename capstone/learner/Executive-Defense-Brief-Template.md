# Executive Defense Brief Template

**Time:** 10 minutes presentation + 10 minutes questions.

1. Outcome, scope and current readiness recommendation.
2. Three key decisions and trade-offs, with owner/evidence.
3. Critical risks, triggers, contingency and escalation threshold.
4. Quality, vendor acceptance, launch/cutover and operational handover status.
5. Tailoring choice and review triggers.
6. Requested executive decision: Go / Conditional Go / No-go.
