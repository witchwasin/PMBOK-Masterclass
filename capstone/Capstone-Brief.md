# Capstone — Full Project Management Handover and Launch

Choose one Primary Track: **ERP Full Project Management Handover Package** or **Hotel Booking Full Delivery and Launch Package**. Submit links to canonical artifacts from Lessons 02–16; do not copy those artifacts here. Submit a 3–5 page Comparative Tailoring Memo for the other scenario.

## Sequential release gates

1. **Artifact Completeness:** required manifest links, governance role and approval evidence exist; every artifact is at least Usable.
2. **Scenario Consistency:** facts match scenario master; new assumption has label, rationale and validation owner; ERP Working Budget 45 ล้านบาท remains distinct from Funding Envelope 60 ล้านบาท.
3. **Integration:** Business Need through Benefit is traceable; scope, schedule, cost, quality, risk, acceptance and handover do not contradict.
4. **Executive Defense:** deliver a 10-minute brief plus 10-minute questions, explain three decisions/trade-offs, critical risks and readiness recommendation.

Release requires all four gates in sequence, total score at least 80%, no dimension below Usable, and no critical contradiction.

|---|---:|

| Governance, decision and evidence | 25% |
| Integration consistency | 20% |
