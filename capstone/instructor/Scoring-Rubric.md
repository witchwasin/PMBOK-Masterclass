# Capstone Scoring Rubric

| Dimension | Weight | Incomplete | Usable | Professional |
|---|---:|---|---|---|
| Completeness/traceability | 40% | Required link/evidence absent | Canonical artifacts trace to handover | Decision-ready end-to-end evidence |
| Governance/evidence | 25% | Owner/authority ambiguous | Roles and approval evidence clear | Exceptions and trade-offs defensible |
| Integration | 20% | Critical contradiction | Core controls consistent | Cross-artifact consequences managed |
| Comparative tailoring | 15% | Method label only | Context, rationale and review stated | Workstream triggers sustain adaptation |

Pass only at total ≥80%, no dimension below Usable and no critical contradiction.
