# L13 Scoring Rubric

| Level | Evidence |
|---|---|
| Incomplete | Vague risks have no trigger, owner or response. |
| Usable | Risk-to-issue escalation and delegated approvals are traceable. |
| Professional | Thresholds, reserve, governance and change control withstand review. |

Score analytical distinction, governance and evidence equally. Usable is the minimum pass.
