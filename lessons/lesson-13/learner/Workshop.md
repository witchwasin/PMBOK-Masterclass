# L13 Workshop — ERP Data Migration Escalation

**Scenario:** reconciliation defects may prevent cutover. **Role:** PM with data-risk owner.

- Available: red status, draft risk register and agreed escalation path.
- Missing: confidence range, contingency cost and business tolerance.
- Decision: retain as risk, declare issue, escalate, or submit change request.
- Output: one complete register row, response plan and escalation recommendation.
- Constraints: Steering Committee only approves appetite/threshold, high-critical response, excess reserve or escalation.
- Debrief: show the exact trigger that changes risk into issue.
