# L11 Workshop — ERP UAT Capacity Decision

**Scenario:** ERP needs 80 key-user test-days while finance and warehouse retain BAU duties. **Role:** PM.

- Available: draft schedule, named functional managers, RACI.
- Missing: confirmed release capacity and backfill cost.
- Decision: dedicate, phase, or backfill key users; name the approver.
- Output: complete the Resource Plan and one decision brief.
- Constraints: PM cannot approve a functional department's resource commitment.
- Debrief: What is a role, person, assignment and capacity? What evidence makes the plan Usable?
