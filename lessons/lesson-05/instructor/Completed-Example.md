# Completed Example — ERP Integration Pack

## Charter Extract

- Business Need: รวมข้อมูล/process เพื่อ real-time visibility และลดเวลาปิดงบ 15 → 5 วัน
- Scope: Finance, Procurement, Inventory, Production Planning และ HCM
- Out of Scope: CRM, BI และ Field Sales Mobile
- Working Budget: 45 ล้านบาท; Total Funding Envelope: 60 ล้านบาท
- Sponsor: CEO; Benefit Owner: CFO/Process Owners
- PM Authority: บริหารตาม approved baselines และ escalate decisions เกิน threshold

## Governance Extract

| Decision | Reviewer | Approval Authority | Evidence |
|---|---|---|---|
| Charter/baseline | PMO and Business Owners | Sponsor/Steering Committee | Approved Charter/baseline |
| Change 2 ล้านบาท + 3 สัปดาห์ | Impact Owners | Steering Committee | Decision record |
| Operational acceptance | Business/IT Operations | Business Owner | Acceptance and handover record |

## Change Decision Extract

- Request: เพิ่ม Approval Workflow
- Options: include now; defer post-go-live; replace lower-value scope
- Missing evidence: regulatory deadline, acceptance criteria, SI estimate basis, UAT/data readiness
- Recommendation: defer pending regulatory evidence; if mandatory, approve only with re-baselined UAT/cutover and named acceptance owner
- Evidence: Steering Committee decision and updated scope/schedule/cost baselines
