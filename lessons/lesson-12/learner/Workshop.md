# L12 Workshop — Make the ERP Status Actionable

**Scenario:** data migration is red, but the weekly email shows only a percentage complete. **Role:** PM.

- Available: RACI, current status, data-quality finding.
- Missing: recovery options and decision date.
- Decision: what brief goes to whom, through which cadence, and when to escalate.
- Output: communication-plan row and one executive status/decision brief.
- Constraints: Sponsor approves the plan/cadence; PM approves the recurring status report.
- Debrief: distinguish data, information, communication, status and a decision brief.
