# L14 Workshop — Review an ERP SI Change Request

**Scenario:** system integrator requests time-and-material payment for a customization with unclear scope boundary. **Role:** PM and Procurement Manager.

- Available: SOW excerpt, vendor proposal, L13 change evidence.
- Missing: acceptance criteria and authority limit.
- Decision: reject, clarify and re-price, or approve a controlled change.
- Output: contract-control record, acceptance condition and recommendation.
- Constraints: Legal, Finance, IT and Business Owners review; PM cannot approve commercial authority beyond delegation.
- Debrief: separate contract deliverable, business acceptance, payment and closure.
