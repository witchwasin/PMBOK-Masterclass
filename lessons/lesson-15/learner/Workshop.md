# L15 Workshop — Protect the Hotel Booking Sprint Goal

**Scenario:** a payment risk threatens MVP checkout while marketing requests a promotion feature. **Role:** Product Owner and Delivery Team.

- Available: backlog, board, payment-risk evidence and Definition of Done.
- Missing: conversion impact and release deadline tolerance.
- Decision: reprioritise, split, pause flow or change release scope.
- Output: updated backlog, board policy and feedback request.
- Constraints: PO owns priority; Team owns the board; Release Authority decides launch.
- Debrief: distinguish product backlog/WBS, sprint/phase, and increment/activity output.
